MEET THE TEAM


Karakorum Expeditions Pvt Ltd. has been training High Altitude Guides in Pakistan for decades and formed Pakistan Mountaineering association. Also, Mirza Ali is the first Pakistani man to climb seven summits in seven continents.


MIRZA ALI

Chairman and Team Leader

Mirza Ali is the First Pakistani Man to Complete Seven Summits in Seven Continents. He has been training high altitude guides in order to impart them in polishing their skills in winter and aim for high mountains. Mirza Ali has been guided to seven summits, K2, Broad Peak, Nanga Parbat, GI, GII in Pakistan, Mt. Everest, and Manasulu in Nepal.

GUL MUHAMMAD

Director

Gul Muhammad has been in tourism industry for the last 22 years, and has cater more than 100+ groups to different climbing expeditions and treks in Pakistan. He is well aware of all the terrains, he is also dealing with so many different cultures, wild life tours.
